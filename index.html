About me

Being a developer at the start can feel like diving into a vast ocean, where every day brings a new wave of challenges. But if you stick with it, pushing through the basics and embracing the learning curve, you’ll find that the possibilities are truly endless. Heya! I am Kre0lidge (or Kreee) and this is my site to showcase projects I will be making for the duration of my journey! I have a keen interest in JavaScript and web development, trying different frameworks like React, Angular, Vue etc. Each of them have their own annoyances and cool things to do with, but this is exactly something that keeps me on my toes and makes every little task challenging and exciting. With all this, I've also made progress in learning more about cloud technologies, especially Kubernetes for managing apps on the cloud. Learning about Docker, CI/CD, AWS, and Azure platforms is quite fascinating. It’s a lot to juggle, but every little win, every new discovery, makes it all worthwhile. It’s messy, it’s hard, but I have absolutely been loving every bit of it. Hit me up on Discord and check out my GitHub for when I post cool stuff!
